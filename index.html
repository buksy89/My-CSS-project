About

DIFF Medical Centre is a renowned healthcare institution located in Abuja, Nigeria. Let me provide you with some details about this esteemed medical center: History and Leadership: Managed by Dr. John Onaiwu Idahosa, DIFF Medical Centre has been serving patients for several years. Dr. Idahosa is a highly experienced physician and a registered member of the American Society for Reproductive Medicine (ASRM) and the Society for Human Reproductive and Embryology (ESHRE). The hospital’s inception was inspired by the birth of a set of triplets after nine years of infertility. These three children—Divine, Favour, and Faith—form the acronym for DIFF.
